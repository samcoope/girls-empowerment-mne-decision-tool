How to Use This Decision Making Tool

This tool helps you select appropriate monitoring and evaluation (M&E) methodologies for adolescent girls empowerment programs based on your specific context, resources, and measurement goals.


Using This Tool

Follow these steps to find the best methodologies for your needs:

  1. Select SEM Level: Choose which level of the socio-ecological model your M&E focuses on (Individual, Interpersonal, Community, or Institutional).
  2. Select Context: Indicate factors about your operating environment such as stability, cultural context, and political sensitivity.
  3. Select Resources: Specify what resources you have available in terms of funding, skills, and technology.
  4. View Results: See methodologies that match your selections, explore their details, and download the results.

Tips for Best Results

  • Start with your most important criteria - you don't need to select options in every category.
  • If you get too few results, try removing some of your selections.
  • If you get too many results, add more criteria to narrow down your options.
  • Click on methodology names to see detailed information about each one.
  • The "Download Results" button creates an HTML file with your selections and matching methodologies.
